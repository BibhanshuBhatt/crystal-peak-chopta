About Us

Your Himalayan
Sanctuary Awaits

Nestled at 2,680 metres in the Garhwal Himalayas, Crystal Peak Chopta blends raw mountain wilderness with thoughtful comfort. We sit just 1 km from Tungnath Temple — the world's highest Shiva shrine — making us the ideal basecamp for Chandrashila, Deoria Tal, and Rohini Bugyal treks.

Every tent, every meal, every bonfire evening is curated to give you an authentic Himalayan experience without sacrificing the comforts that make a stay truly restful.

Directions

How to Reach Chopta

1

Reach Haridwar / Rishikesh

Fly to Dehradun (Jolly Grant Airport) or take a train to Haridwar / Rishikesh from Delhi or any major city. Overnight buses also available.

2

Drive to Ukhimath / Rudraprayag

Take a bus or shared cab from Rishikesh to Ukhimath (~180 km, 6–7 hrs via Devprayag & Rudraprayag). Private cabs widely available.

3

Ukhimath → Chopta (25 km)

From Ukhimath, Chopta is just 25 km (~1 hour drive). Local jeeps and shared taxis ply this route regularly.

4

We'll Guide You In

Crystal Peak Camp is at the Chopta meadow entrance, 1 km before Tungnath trailhead. WhatsApp us for live directions on arrival.
